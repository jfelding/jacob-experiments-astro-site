## Why is silicone so special?

* Endures both high and low temperatures
* Can be foodgrade, skin etc.
* Available in many consistencies
* Can be used as a sealant
* Can be used as glue
* Can be used in a casting process, in combination with 3D printing

## Project ideas for food-grade silicone:

* Casting own kitchen utensils
* Repairing electric kettles, blenders, etc.

## Needs

* Vacuum chamber and pump for degassing
* A variety of silicone
